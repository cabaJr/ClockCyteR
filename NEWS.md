# clockcyteR 0.1.0

* Initial release.
* Shiny app for circadian rhythm analysis of Incucyte timeseries data.
* Supports FFT-NLLS, chi-square, autocorrelation, Lomb-Scargle, Fourier, and CWT periodogram methods.
* Interactive timeseries viewer with detrending and outlier removal.
* Period, amplitude, RAE, and phase scatter plots with cross-plot selection.
* Rayleigh circular plot for phase analysis.
* Group-level summary statistics table.
* Sample exclusion from downstream analysis.
* FFT-NLLS fitted curve overlay on timeseries.
